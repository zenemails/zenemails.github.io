About Nicole

I’ve always been curious about why people make the choices they do.

What makes someone trust a brand? What turns a first interaction into a lasting relationship? What small moments influence whether someone clicks, returns, subscribes, or walks away?

My career has been built around answering those questions.

With 10+ years of experience across customer experience, CRM, lifecycle marketing, and digital strategy, I’ve worked with global brands including Disney, Sony, and Toyota to understand audiences, design meaningful customer journeys, and create experiences that connect people with the brands they love.

Over time, I realized the part of my work I enjoyed most wasn’t just launching campaigns — it was uncovering the insights behind them. Listening to customers. Finding patterns in behavior. Turning research into strategy. Connecting business goals with human needs.

Today, I approach experience design through a blend of UX research, customer psychology, lifecycle strategy, and storytelling. I believe the best experiences happen when we take the time to understand people first.

When I’m not mapping customer journeys or analyzing behavior, you’ll probably find me exploring new places, taking photos, reading a psychological thriller, or noticing the small details that make everyday experiences memorable.

I’m Nicole — a customer experience strategist who believes better experiences begin with better understanding.
